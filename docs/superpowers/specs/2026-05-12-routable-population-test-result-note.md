# The routable-population test вҖ” result: no routable population; the per-case routing null is robust six ways over; and the "perfect-by-letter-grade" split is de-dup-definition-dependent

**Date:** 2026-05-12.
**Status:** Result. Pre-registration: `docs/superpowers/specs/2026-05-12-routable-population-test-preregistration-note.md` (commit `1cc7d87`, OTS `13aecfa`) вҖ” committed before any experimental code touched the data. Script `scripts/routable_population_test.py`; data `runs/routable_population_test_results.json`; reuses `wedge/refinement_set.py` + `wedge/routing.py` wholesale (no new `wedge/` code, no new unit tests вҖ” 235 pass, 1 skipped, unchanged).
**One line.** Per-case within-Rashomon disagreement-routing is **dead, robustly**: it does not localize the borrowers on which the band consensus is least reliable вҖ” not on the #6 named-only band, not on the extension-admitted band de-duped by tree signature, not on the extension-admitted band de-duped by used-feature-set, and not under any of three bucketings (raw `d_ext`, the policy-blind residual `r = d_ext вҲ’ dМӮ_named`, the policy-explained part `dМӮ_named`). The "routing booleans" that flicker True on 2вҖ“3 of 4 subprime grades fire on Brier/ECE differences of order 10вҒ»ВівҖ“10вҒ»ВІ on baselines of 0.13вҖ“0.22, with no sign-consistency across bucketings or grades вҖ” noise, not signal. **And** the extension-admitted result's "the disagreement collapses off the policy vocabulary, perfectly by letter grade" is **de-dup-definition-dependent**: under the more policy-meaningful "distinct refinement = distinct feature set" de-dup, **C1 recovers named-legibility** (`RВІ_named` 0.06 вҶ’ 0.40 вҖ” its "collapse" was std-over-271-near-duplicate-trees picking up threshold-sensitivity noise the named features can't track); only C2/C5/D4 genuinely collapse. So the routing claim, in the regulator deliverable, is now **closed** at the case level вҖ” it stands only as *observability* (the documented policy's vocabulary is inadequate to the indeterminacy on tiers C2/C5/D4) and *aggregate, tier-by-tier model-risk surveillance*, not triage.

## 1. What ran

LC 36-month, Burst D (the load-bearing 2015H2 `dti` burst). Bands built on **VвӮҒ = 2015Q3** (73 567 loans), routing metrics forward-evaluated on **VвӮӮ = 2015Q4** (88 669); VвӮҒ-in-sample arm reported. All 7 flagged Cat-2-(pricing) grades on 2015Q3 (`A1, A5, B1, C1, C2, C5, D4`); subprime set (= `well_explained = False` in the extension-admitted-band test) = `{C1, C2, C5, D4}`; primary aggregate P = those of {C1,C2,C5,D4} plural under the de-duped band on VвӮҒ = **{C1, C2, C5, D4}** (all four plural). Policy `policy/thin_demo_hmda.yaml`.

**Band construction:** identical to the extension-admitted test (`build_refinement_band` over the 13-feature candidate set named вҲӘ extension, monotonicity `{fico_range_low:вҲ’1, dti:+1}` on the named features only, Оө = 0.02, depths {1,2,3}, leaf-mins {25,50,100}, seed 0, train/holdout 0.3) **except** the within-Оө members are then re-de-duplicated by **used-feature-set** (the feature set the train-split tree splits on, read off `tree_signature`), keeping the highest-holdout-AUC representative per feature set вҖ” the band-bloat fix, and the more principled "distinct refinement" (the policy is a *vocabulary*; a refinement is a *choice of which words to use*, not a choice of thresholds).

**Disagreement, explainer, residual:** `d_ext(x)` = std over the de-duped refit members' P(default | x). On VвӮҒ: `tree_named` = `DecisionTreeRegressor(max_depth=3, min_samples_leaf=50, random_state=0).fit(XвӮҒ_named, d_ext_v1)`; `dМӮ_named` = its prediction (on VвӮҒ and VвӮӮ); `r = d_ext вҲ’ dМӮ_named`. CV-RВІ of `d_ext` from the named features (`RВІ_named`) and from named вҲӘ extension (`RВІ_all`) via `disagreement_explainer` (depth вүӨ 3, leaf-min 50, 5-fold). Three bucketings of each held-out quarter's borrowers вҖ” terciles of `r` (the policy-blind bucketing, primary), of `d_ext` (raw, control), of `dМӮ_named` (the explained part, negative control). For each: the four #6 routing metrics (`wedge.routing` 1вҖ“4) + the `feature_space_outlierness` diagnostic + the `grade_routing_verdict` booleans. Plus, within the top `d_ext` tercile, a policy-blind half (high `r`) vs named-explained half (low `r`) vs the full complement consensus-reliability contrast (Brier, AUC, ECE, min-over-members Brier per cell).

### 2c. Why the residual decomposition was nearly a no-op on these tiers

Bucketing-agreement (V2): Spearman(`r`-terciles, `d_ext`-terciles) вүҲ **0.79вҖ“0.85** (continuous `r`~`d_ext` вүҲ 0.84вҖ“0.95); Spearman(`dМӮ_named`-terciles, `d_ext`-terciles) вүҲ 0.21вҖ“0.38. So the residual bucketing is ~the raw bucketing relabeled вҖ” the named explainer captures so little of `d_ext` on the subprime tiers (RВІ 0.08вҖ“0.25) that subtracting `dМӮ_named` mostly removes its mean. The `dМӮ_named` bucketing *is* genuinely different from the raw вҖ” but it's the *small* component, and it doesn't route either. The decomposition didn't fail to find a routable population; there wasn't one to find, and the decomposition couldn't have manufactured a meaningfully different test on tiers where the policy vocabulary explains ~10вҖ“25% of the disagreement.

## 3. Verdict

**NO ROUTABLE POPULATION.** Per-case within-Rashomon disagreement-routing is dead, and the null is now **robust across six independent ways of asking**: #6 named-only band (deflated, `2026-05-12-disagreement-routing-result-note.md`); extension-admitted band, tree-sig de-dup, `d_ext` terciles (this is the `raw_d_ext` arm here under that de-dup's spirit вҖ” deflated); extension-admitted band, used-feat de-dup, `d_ext` terciles (deflated); вҖҰ `r` (residual) terciles (deflated); вҖҰ `dМӮ_named` (explained) terciles (deflated); and the within-top-`d_ext`-tercile policy-blind-vs-named-explained contrast (no consistent reliability gap). The mechanism the pre-reg was built to detect вҖ” the band consensus biased toward the base rate where the truth tracks an extension feature, so the policy-blind disagreement marks consensus-unreliability вҖ” **does not appear**: where the disagreement is large the consensus is no worse (and the band members are no better) than where it is small, on these tiers, forward. This is the pre-registered terminal outcome (В§3/В§4 of the pre-reg): "per-case disagreement-routing is dead even with the wider feature view вҖ” the extension-admitted finding is about *observability*, not *triage*." It is **not** a prompt for test #N+1; the case-level-routing door is closed.

**Secondary finding (the one with bite): the extension-admitted "perfect-by-letter-grade collapse" was de-dup-definition-dependent.** Under the policy-meaningful "distinct refinement = distinct feature set" de-dup, C1 recovers named-legibility (RВІ_named 0.06 вҶ’ 0.40); the genuine collapsers are C2/C5/D4 (RВІ_named 0.08вҖ“0.25, RВІ_all 0.51вҖ“0.57). The "blind tiers are exactly the subprime ones" claim from yesterday softens to "three of the four subprime tiers (C2, C5, D4), and the C1 collapse was an artifact of band bloat." De-dup by used-feature-set is now the validated default (more principled, and the runtime fix).

- **P1 вҖ” MISS, cleanly.** Predicted ~40% the aggregate hits; it missed both legs.
